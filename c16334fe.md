# Business Plan Context: Morocco-Produced Atlantic Riviera Clothing Brand

## Brand Thesis

Build a clothing brand that blends the men's aesthetic of Casatlantic with the women's aesthetic of JLUXLABEL.

The brand should feel like:

- 1960s Casablanca
- Atlantic/Mediterranean resort life
- elevated but accessible luxury
- old-world tailoring
- relaxed sensuality
- warm editorial storytelling
- Morocco production provenance
- modern DTC commerce

The brand should be built as both:

1. A real fashion brand.
2. A technology-enabled operating system for apparel production, tech packs, costing, and supplier management.

## Production Strategy

Keep production in Morocco, ideally Casablanca, wherever Casatlantic produces.

### Preferred Production Hub

Casablanca, Morocco.

Rationale:

- Authentic connection to Casatlantic inspiration
- Strong textile/apparel infrastructure
- Boutique atelier production available
- Better fit for tailored trousers and designer small-batch runs than giant high-volume factories
- Strong brand storytelling value

## Tariff and Duty Context

### EU

Morocco-origin qualifying apparel can potentially enter EU with 0% duty under EU-Morocco trade arrangements.

Application should model this as an editable rule, not hardcoded legal truth.

### US

US-Morocco FTA has yarn-forward rules for many textile/apparel categories.

If the fabric is not Morocco/US origin, many goods may not qualify and may fall back to MFN duty.

Application should model estimated US fallback duty ranges such as 16.5% to 32% by product category, with strong disclaimer that final classification requires trade/legal review.

### System Requirement

The app should have an editable duty and landed cost engine.

No duty calculation should be presented as legal advice.
